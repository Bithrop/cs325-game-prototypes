Peas was never an option

  • Originality: You fight off birds from your crops in waves, not sure about another game that does those two things in one
  • Technical Merit: Any game that has waves of fighting off enemies.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Learned how to use basic x an y values for the things I needed Easiest thing was the movment while the hardest was getting the eggs to reset everytime you picked them up.
  • Prototype Assets: Fight off birds trying to get your crops as you build defenses.
    • Farmer: farmer
    • Farm background: Farm
    • Prototype Closest Other Game: Using privioues game.
    • High Concept: Fight off birds trying to get your crops as you build defenses.
    • Theme: Farmer proctecing his crops.
    • Mandated Variety: Keyboard, plenty as the egg spots are random, adventrue, and collector/achiever
    • Prototype Goal: Waves of enemies and tile mapping for the different defenses.
    • Player Experience Goals: Want them to try to get the highest of number of waves cleared as the waves continue to grow and they lose more crops..
    • Gameplay: The player can use the arrow keys to move around the map and spacebar to shoot their gun at any birds. Each wave will have more birds come to get the crops which are at the center of the screen. In between rounds you will have options to build defenses and upgrades to help fend off these birds. Depending on how many crops you still have left in between each round will determine how many defenses and/or upgrades you get.
    • Strategies: None right now.
    • Story/Setting/Premise: You’re a farmer on their last legs. Each season has been tough for you but with these special crops that you found at the store you should be able to make a profit each day. However, these crops also attract flocks upon flocks of birds to eat them. You’ll be out in the field each day making sure to keep your crops safe as the crops determine if you have food on the plate each morning.
    • Target Audience: People who like to strategize and people who like games about reflexes.
